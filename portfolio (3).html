Running Footwear February 2026 PM

Bob Marley × adidas Running

Partner: Bob Marley Estate — Kingston, Jamaica

A Jamaican-inspired reinterpretation of the Adizero Evo SL — bringing rhythm, colour, and cultural identity to one of adidas Running's fastest silhouettes. Two colourways: a bold yellow upper with Rastafarian red, black and green Three Stripes, and a blacked-out version with rasta accent detailing. Both feature a "Football Is Freedom" tongue patch alongside Marley's portrait and signature — marking a shift from lifestyle nostalgia into modern performance territory.

Drove global product marketing for the Bob Marley × adidas Running Evo SL as PM for Running Collaborations & Partnerships
Managed full creation cycle from brief through global February 2026 launch on adidas.com and select performance retailers
Aligned partner communication with the Marley estate across Design, Comms, and Development
Coordinated campaign rollout including Lando Norris global campaign activation

Running Footwear/Apparel April 2026

Song for the Mute × adidas Running — "The First Breath"

Partner: Song for the Mute (Lyna Ty & Melvin Tanaya) — Sydney

The first-ever performance running collection from Sydney-based label Song for the Mute and adidas Running. Titled "The First Breath," the collection reframes performance as something instinctive and personal — capturing fog-heavy mornings and the rawness of beginnings. Centred on a reworked Supernova Rise 3 and the Adi365 apparel system.

Worked on global product marketing strategy for the first-ever SFTM × adidas Running performance launch
Drove the Supernova Rise 3 and Adi365 apparel system from creative brief to global April 2, 2026 drop
Coordinated global distribution across adidas.com, SFTM, and premium retail partners worldwide
Translated the label's avant-garde design language into a coherent product marketing narrative
